# Raynbow Restorers

## Perth Powder Coating & Metal Finishing Specialists Since 2000

**Durable Finishes. Professional Results. Trusted Across Western Australia.**

Raynbow Restorers is a Perth-based metal finishing company specialising in powder coating, sandblasting, rust removal, aluminium chroming, metal painting, and specialised coating solutions. Operating from Osborne Park since 2000, we help residential, commercial, industrial, and automotive clients restore, protect, and enhance metal surfaces with long-lasting finishes.

## Sandblasting Services

Proper surface preparation is essential for coating performance and longevity.

Our sandblasting services remove:

* Rust
* Old Paint
* Scale
* Corrosion
* Surface Contaminants

This creates the ideal profile for superior coating adhesion.

➡️ Learn more about our [Sandblasting Services](https://raynbows.com.au/services/sandblasting/).

---

## Rust Removal

Rust can dramatically reduce the lifespan of valuable metal assets. Our professional rust removal process restores metal surfaces and prepares them for long-term protection.

Many clients combine rust removal with our [Powder Coating Services](https://raynbows.com.au/services/powder-coating/) for maximum durability.
